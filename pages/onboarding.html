Welcome to SessionLink

Never lose your AI conversation context again. Save, summarize, and resume your chats seamlessly.

1

Get Your API Key

SessionLink uses AI to summarize your conversations. You'll need an API key from OpenAI or Google Gemini.

2

Configure the Extension

Click the SessionLink icon in your browser toolbar, select your API provider, and paste your API key. Your key is stored locally and never leaves your device.

3

Save Your Context

When you're in a ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini conversation, click the floating "Save State" button to capture and summarize your current context.

4

Resume Anytime

Starting a new chat? Click "Resume State" to instantly inject your saved context and continue right where you left off.
